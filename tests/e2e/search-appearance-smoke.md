# Search Appearance Module Smoke

1. Open `Open Growth SEO > Search Appearance`.
2. Set global title/meta templates and save.
3. Configure at least one CPT template and robots default, save, and reopen to verify persistence.
4. Configure taxonomy robots defaults and archive robots defaults.
5. Verify SERP preview updates in real time while typing templates and title separator.
6. Verify social card preview updates in real time and reflects OG/Twitter enable/disable states.
7. Verify rich result hints are informative and non-promissory (eligibility hints, no guarantees).
8. Verify preview microcopy clearly states it is indicative only.
9. Open a frontend singular page and verify title/meta description output reflects template or per-post override.
10. Verify canonical output exists only once and respects per-post canonical override.
11. Verify robots meta output follows per-post override; otherwise context defaults.
12. Activate a known SEO plugin with safe mode enabled and verify OGS meta output is suppressed.
13. Deactivate other SEO plugin and verify OGS output resumes.
