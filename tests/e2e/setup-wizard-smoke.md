# Setup Wizard Module Smoke

1. Open `Open Growth SEO > Setup Wizard` on a clean activation.
2. Verify Step 1 shows detections: site type, WooCommerce, language(s), SEO plugins, indexability, robots.txt.
3. Continue to Step 2 and switch between Simple/Advanced.
4. Set visibility to `private` without confirmation and verify inline validation blocks progression.
5. Enable private confirmation and continue to Step 3.
6. Click Back and verify selections persist.
7. Click Pause Wizard, reopen wizard, verify draft resumes at previous step.
8. Click Restart and verify wizard resets to Step 1 defaults.
9. Complete Apply Configuration and verify redirect to dashboard with success notice.
10. Reopen Setup Wizard after completion and verify re-entry notice appears.
11. With another SEO plugin active, verify safe mode is enforced in Step 2.
12. In multisite, verify copy states setup applies to current site only.
